Basic makeup

Basic makeup is the process of using essential cosmetic products to evening out your complexion, add subtle color, and enhance your natural facial features.

HD- Makeup

HD Makeup (High Definition Makeup) is a professional technique using ultra-fine, light-diffusing cosmetic products to create a flawless, camera-ready finish that looks completely natural under high-resolution lenses.

Inquire

Premium makeup

Premium makeup (often called luxury or high-end makeup) refers to prestige cosmetics formulated with superior-quality ingredients, advanced skincare technologies, and rich pigments to deliver an elevated performance and an indulgent sensory experience.

Inquire

Ultra HD makeup

Ultra HD makeup uses specialized micronized pigments and advanced 3D polymer networks. This technique coats the skin in a weightless, hyper-realistic layer that covers severe imperfections without showing a single trace of product texture, even during micro-zoomed close-ups
